What is Catalyst Cash?

Catalyst Cash is a privacy-first AI finance app that gives you the kind of advice usually reserved for people with financial advisors — except it's instant, automatic, and lives on your iPhone.

Privacy-First

Your core financial data is stored locally on your device. AI requests are proxied securely only when you run audits or chat.

AI-Powered

Your AI coach reads your full financial picture — every balance, bill, and card — then gives you specific, actionable advice tailored to your exact situation.

Fast & Secure

AI requests are routed through the Catalyst Cash backend proxy with PII scrubbing before reaching the selected model provider. Raw financial payloads are not retained on our servers.

What You Can Track

The more Catalyst knows about your finances, the smarter its advice gets. Here's everything you can add to your portfolio:

  • Bank Accounts (Checking & Savings): Connect via Plaid for automatic balance updates, or enter them manually. Catalyst monitors your cash flow and spending runway.
  • Credit Cards: Track every card — balances, limits, APRs, annual fees, and due dates. Catalyst flags utilization spikes and finds optimization opportunities.
  • Debts & Loans: Add auto loans, student loans, mortgages, or personal debt. The built-in Debt Simulator shows you exactly how to pay them off fastest.
  • Investments: Sync brokerage, Roth IRA, and 401(k) accounts via Plaid — or add them manually — to see your complete net worth in one view.
  • Crypto: Add your crypto holdings with live market prices pulled automatically.
  • Other Assets: Track real estate, vehicles, valuables — anything with a dollar value. Classify each as Liquid or Locked.

How It Works: The Safety Floor

Set a minimum "Emergency Floor" for your checking account. If your upcoming bills would push you below that floor, Catalyst warns you immediately so you can adjust before it's too late.

How It Works: Smart Debt Payoff

The Debt Simulator automatically orders your debts by highest interest rate first (the "avalanche" method). This is mathematically proven to save the most money on interest over time.

Your Weekly Routine

Spend 5 minutes each week to stay on top of your money. Here's the simple routine that makes Catalyst work best:

1

Sync Your Accounts

If you use Plaid, tap Sync — your balances and recent transactions update automatically. Otherwise, quickly update your checking balance and any card balances that changed.

2

Run Your Check-In

Tap the Audit button. Catalyst AI will analyze your cash flow, upcoming bills, credit utilization, and spending pace — then give you a clear action plan.

3

Review Your Results

Your Dashboard updates with your health score, available spending money, and any alerts. Green means you're on track. Red means take action now.

4

Ask Follow-Up Questions

Need more detail? Open Ask AI to chat directly. Ask things like "Should I pay off my Visa or my car loan first?" and get instant, personalized advice.

Your Chats Auto-Delete

For your privacy, all AI chat conversations are automatically deleted after 24 hours. Your financial data is scrubbed from chat history before any processing occurs.

Privacy & Backup

You have complete control over your data. Catalyst gives you multiple ways to back up, export, and protect your finances.

iCloud Cross-Device Backup

Your data is automatically backed up to your private iCloud container. Switch devices or sign in on a new phone and your data restores automatically — no manual import needed.

Spreadsheet Export

Export your entire portfolio as an .xlsx spreadsheet. Edit in Excel or Google Sheets, then import it back into the app.

Encrypted Backup

Create a password-protected .enc backup file. Store it in iCloud Drive, AirDrop it to a new phone, or save it anywhere you trust. Passcodes, API keys, device IDs, and subscription state are excluded automatically.

Built with Your Trust in Mind

Catalyst Cash is not a financial advisor. All AI insights are for informational purposes only. We do not store raw financial payloads on our servers, and your core financial data remains stored locally on your device.

Reset & Delete

Clear History

Deletes all past AI check-in results. Your accounts and settings are kept.

Factory Reset

Permanently erases all data — accounts, saved credentials, history, and settings. The app resets to its first-launch state. Back up first.
