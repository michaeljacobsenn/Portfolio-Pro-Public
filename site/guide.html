How Catalyst Cash Works

Catalyst Cash is more than a budgeting app. It's a complete financial toolkit designed to help you take full control of your money, grow your wealth, and build lasting financial health.

Private by Design

Your financial data stays on your device. Your balances, accounts, and personal details never touch our servers. Everything is processed locally on your phone.

Your Complete Financial Picture

The more your AI coach knows about your finances, the better advice it can give. Catalyst Cash tracks all your accounts in one place.

  • Checking & Savings: Connect your bank accounts via Plaid for automatic balance updates, or enter them manually.
  • Investment Accounts: Sync your brokerage, Roth IRA, 401(k), and HSA accounts via Plaid. Track holdings with live market prices.
  • Credit Cards: Add your cards with limits, APRs, annual fees, and due dates. See your total utilization and upcoming payments at a glance.
  • Debts & Savings Goals: Track non-card debts (mortgage, auto, student loans) and set savings targets — all in the Accounts tab.
  • Crypto & Other Assets: Add crypto holdings with live market prices and track other assets like real estate or vehicles.

Smart Rule: Emergency Floor

Set a minimum checking balance you never want to go below. If upcoming bills would push you below this safety net, Catalyst highlights the risk and suggests adjustments.

Smart Rule: Debt Payoff Optimizer

The Debt Simulator ranks all your debts and finds the fastest payoff order — avalanche (highest interest first) or snowball (smallest balance first) — so you save the most money.

Your Weekly Check-In

Spend 5 minutes every Sunday running through these steps to stay on top of your finances and catch problems before they grow.

1

Sync Your Accounts

Tap "Sync latest" to pull live balances from Plaid, or update your manual accounts with this week's numbers.

2

Run Your Audit

Tap one button and your AI coach analyzes everything — spending, debts, bills, and investments — to generate your personalized action plan.

3

Review Your Dashboard

Your dashboard updates instantly with charts, health scores, net worth trends, and a clear breakdown of where your money is going.

4

Ask Follow-Up Questions

Need more detail? Open Ask AI and chat with your coach. It knows your full financial picture and can answer anything from "Can I afford this?" to "How do I save more?"

Your Privacy Is Protected

All chat messages automatically delete after 24 hours. Before anything is saved locally, sensitive information like account numbers is automatically masked for your protection.

Data Privacy & Backup

You have complete control over your data. Catalyst Cash provides secure backup and export tools so your information is always safe.

Transparent & Honest

No hidden fees, no dark patterns, no misleading claims. Our pricing is clear, and we never guarantee investment returns.

iCloud Cross-Device Backup

Your data is automatically backed up to your private iCloud container. Get a new phone? Just sign in with your Apple ID and everything restores automatically.

Export Your Data

Download your financial data as CSV or PDF reports. Your data belongs to you — take it anywhere, anytime.

Encrypted Backups

Create AES-256 encrypted backups of your data. Store them in iCloud or anywhere you choose — only you can decrypt them with your passcode.

Danger Zone — Data Deletion

Clear Audit History

Deletes all past audit results while keeping your accounts, settings, and portfolio intact.

Factory Reset

Completely erases all data — accounts, settings, API keys, and history. Returns the app to a fresh install state.
