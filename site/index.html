FAQ

The questions people ask before they trust a finance app.

Do I need to link my bank?

No. Plaid is optional. The app still works with manual balances, debts, and renewals.

What makes this different from budgeting apps?

Budgeting apps mainly track. Catalyst Cash is optimized for a weekly review that tells you what matters now and what to do next.

Is my data stored on your servers?

Your core financial data is stored locally on your device. AI requests go through a secure proxy with scrubbed prompts, and raw financial payloads are not retained on our servers.

Who is this best for?

People with real financial complexity: multiple cards, inconsistent spending, debt payoff goals, subscription creep, or too many accounts to mentally track well.

Privacy + Clarity

Your money data should feel private and actionable.

Catalyst Cash stores your core financial data locally on your device. Manual backups exclude passcodes, API keys, device identifiers, and subscription state. AI requests are routed through the Catalyst Cash backend proxy with PII scrubbing.
