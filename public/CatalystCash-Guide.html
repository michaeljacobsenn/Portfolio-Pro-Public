01 / The Catalyst Philosophy

Catalyst Cash is not just a tracker; it is an active financial co-pilot. To get the most out of it, shift your mindset from "logging transactions" to "strategic forecasting."

Golden Rule: The Safety Floor

Go to your Profile and set an absolute Emergency Floor. This is your untouchable baseline. Catalyst AI is highly tuned to detect any upcoming expenses or trends that threaten to breach this floor.

  • Forward-Looking: We care more about where your cash flow will be in 30 days than where it was 30 days ago.
  • Zero-Based Budgeting: Input all your renewals and fixed bills to achieve zero "Unassigned Cash." Catalyst mathematically scores your health.
  • Action-Oriented: Use 1-Click Cancel links on the Sinking Funds tab to quickly eliminate unused subscriptions.

02 / The Weekly Sync Routine

Your AI is only as smart as the data it sees. Make this 60-second routine a habit every Friday or on your Payday.

1

Refresh Plaid Links

Tap the 'Sync Balances' bar on the Dashboard. This pulls the freshest ledger data directly from your institutions securely.

2

Log Heavy Hitters

If you made a massive manual purchase (e.g., booked a flight on a non-synced card), add it to your Expenses tab so the AI can account for cash drain.

3

Run the Weekly Audit

Tap "Run New Audit". Catalyst will analyze your pace, flag subscription spikes, and project your 30-day runway.

03 / Mastering AskAI

The "AskAI" tab is a direct line to a financial model that has your entire portfolio context pre-loaded. Here is how to use it optimally:

Scenario Testing

Instead of generic questions, ask specific what-ifs: "If I put \$1,500 toward the Chase Sapphire today, how does that affect my emergency floor next week given rent is due?"

Audit Drill-Downs

Tap a specific insight from your weekly audit report and hit "Discuss". The AI will instantly branch into a conversation about that exact problem.

05 / The Ledger / Subscription Trap

The number one cause of lifestyle creep is forgotten renewals.

  1. Go to the Input tab and select "Renewal / Bill".
  2. Log every single streaming service, software, and utility.
  3. Catalyst will aggregate these into a monthly "Sinking Fund" requirement on your dashboard, showing you the true baseline cost of your lifestyle.

Pro Tip: Look at your "Lifestyle Creep Rate" widget on the dashboard. If it turns orange, you are adding subscriptions faster than your income justifies.

06 / Household Sync (E2EE)

Combining finances with a partner? Catalyst Cash allows you to seamlessly link devices.

Zero Knowledge Encryption

Your data is encrypted on your device before ever touching the cloud. Only you and anyone with your shared passcode can unlock it.

  1. Head to the Settings tab and open Backup & Sync.
  2. Tap Setup under Household Sync.
  3. Enter a unique Household ID and a strong Passcode. Share this with your partner.
  4. Once they enter the exact same ID and Passcode, your ledgers will automatically merge and sync.
