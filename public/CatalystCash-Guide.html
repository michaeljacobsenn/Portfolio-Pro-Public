Security & Fast Access

The Security Suite protects your net worth and private rules using native iOS/Mac biometrics.

  • Passcode Setup: Go to Settings → Require Passcode. Create a 4-digit PIN. This is the fail-safe if biometrics fail.
  • Biometric Enrollment: Toggle Enable Face ID. The system will trigger a native iOS prompt to link your biometrics to the application state.
  • Relock Precision: Adjust the Relock After timer. Options range from Immediately (Max Security) to 1 Hour (Workflow Efficiency).

05 /

Building Your Portfolio

A high-fidelity audit requires accurate card and subscription data. Initialize these first.

  • Card Portfolio: Add cards with their Credit Limit and APR. Toggle Waived? if you have a first-year-free promotion active.
  • Renewals & Bills: Group items into Categories (Fixed, Subs, Sinking Funds). Use the Charged To selector to link bills to specific cards.
  • Vaults & Sinking Funds: Define your long-term needs in the Tracker. The AI uses this to calculate your weekly pacing requirement.

Promo APR Support

Detected 0% APR offers are tracked with expiration dates to ensure they are cleared before the interest-clock expires.

Waived Fee Logic

Cards marked "Waived" are excluded from total annual fee projections until you manually toggle the status off.

06 /

Data Governance

You own your data. Catalyst Cash provides tools for backup, migration, and purging.

File System

Data automatically saves securely on your device. No cloud sync required.

JSON Export

Tap EXPORT JSON to save a human readable file of your entire system state to your device.

The Restore

Tap RESTORE and select a previous JSON backup to instantly rebuild your portfolio.

Danger Zone — Irreversible Actions

Clear Audit History

Deletes only the snapshots. Keeps cards, settings, and renewals intact.

Factory Reset

Wipes 100% of data. Returns the application to a blank state.

⚠️ Critical Pre-Requisite: Configure Your Profile

Before running your first audit, it is mandatory to fully configure your Financial Profile in the Settings tab. The AI engine requires exact inputs regarding your strict weekly spending limits, savings targets, and debt allocations to provide mathematical accuracy. If your profile is incomplete, the system will reject or miscalculate your audit.
