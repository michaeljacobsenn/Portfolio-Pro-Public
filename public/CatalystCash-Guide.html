Building Your Ledger

A high-fidelity AI audit demands exact constraints. The more data you map, the higher the intelligence output.

  • Income & Budgets: Map your Income Sources (Salary, Contracts, Dividends) and set maximum weekly thresholds in your Budget Categories.
  • Card Portfolio: Add cards detailing their Limit and APR. The AI maps utilization strictly against your credit health. Toggle Waived to ignore 0% APR promo fees.
  • Renewals Hub: Log every subscription and recurring bill. Assign exactly which card gets hit. The AI calculates rolling 7-day obligations.
  • Assets & Liabilities: Connect your Brokerage, 401(k), Crypto, and Non-Card Debts (Mortgage, Auto, Student). Liquid and illiquid capital are separated for safety calculations.
